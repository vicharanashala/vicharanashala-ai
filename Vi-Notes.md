# Vi-Notes: Authenticity Verification Platform for Human-Written Content

## Overview

Vi-Notes is an authenticity verification platform designed to distinguish human-written content from AI-generated or AI-assisted text. As AI tools become more common in content creation, establishing trust in authorship is increasingly important. Vi-Notes addresses this by analyzing not just the final output, but the entire writing process, while providing users with a clean and distraction-free writing environment.

## What We Aim to Achieve

Vi-Notes aims to restore trust in digital writing by enabling individuals to prove authorship with confidence. The platform supports academic integrity, professional credibility, and transparent content validation, while continuously adapting to advancements in AI to remain effective over time.

## How It Works

Vi-Notes captures real-time writing behavior such as typing speed, pauses, and editing patterns, which reflect natural human thinking and are difficult for AI to replicate. Alongside this, it analyzes linguistic features like vocabulary diversity and sentence structure. By combining behavioral and textual analysis, the platform detects anomalies such as pasted content, uniform typing patterns, or inconsistencies between writing complexity and revisions.

## Key Features

The platform offers a distraction-free writing interface with background monitoring of keystroke patterns and detection of pasted content. It analyzes both writing behavior and text to generate authenticity scores and provides shareable reports that validate authorship in academic and professional contexts.

## Technology Stack

Vi-Notes is built using React, TypeScript, and Electron for the frontend, with Node.js and Express.js on the backend. Machine learning models using TensorFlow and PyTorch handle classification and anomaly detection, while NLP techniques analyze text patterns. Data is securely stored in MongoDB with encryption.

## Detection Philosophy

The platform is based on the idea that human writing is naturally variable and imperfect, while AI-generated content tends to be more consistent and lacks behavioral context. By focusing on how content is written, Vi-Notes provides a more reliable approach to authenticity verification.

## Current Development Status

Vi-Notes is currently under development with core features such as a writing editor, user authentication, keystroke timing capture, pasted content detection, and secure session storage already in place.

## Future Vision

Vi-Notes aims to become a standard solution for authorship verification across education, recruitment, and publishing. The platform will continue evolving to keep pace with advancements in AI and maintain trust in written content.
