*Like a pulse that reveals what lies within,*
*Spandan listens, questions, and brings learning to life from within.*

Officially developed at the Vicharanashala Lab, Indian Institute of Technology Ropar, Spandan is an AI-powered, open-source classroom engagement system designed to transform how interaction happens in live teaching environments. Built with a vision to make engagement seamless and continuous, the platform integrates real-time speech processing with intelligent question generation to ensure that learning is not just delivered—but experienced.

---

## **About**

Spandan is a real-time engagement automation system that converts live teaching into an interactive learning experience. It listens to the instructor’s speech, transcribes it instantly, and generates context-aware questions that reflect the content being taught at that very moment.

Unlike traditional systems where interaction is manually inserted, Spandan embeds engagement directly into the flow of teaching. It creates a learning environment where students are continuously thinking, responding, and reflecting—while instructors focus solely on teaching.

---

## **The Challenge**

In both online and physical classrooms, one fundamental challenge persists:  
*How do we know if students are truly engaged while teaching is happening?*

Traditional solutions such as polls or quizzes introduce friction:
- They interrupt the natural teaching flow  
- They require pre-planning or additional manpower  
- They fail to adapt dynamically to live content  

As observed in real classroom settings, students often remain passive—cameras off, attention uncertain, and understanding unverified. Even when polls are used, they demand cognitive effort from instructors and disrupt continuity.

The core challenge is not just interaction—but **seamless, real-time, context-aware engagement without disrupting instruction**.

---

## **The Platform**

Spandan addresses this challenge through a continuous AI-driven interaction loop that connects teaching, questioning, and feedback in real time.

**Real-Time Listening**

Spandan passively listens to the instructor using browser-based audio capture. Speech is converted into accurate transcripts using advanced ASR models like Whisper, ensuring that every concept delivered is captured without interrupting the lecture.

**Context-Aware Question Generation**

Using large language models, Spandan generates questions directly from the live transcript. These questions are strictly grounded in the content being taught, ensuring relevance and conceptual alignment.

This transforms every lecture into an opportunity for immediate reflection and understanding.


**Instructor-in-the-Loop**

Spandan keeps instructors at the center of control. Every generated question is presented for quick review, allowing teachers to approve, modify, or discard it before it reaches students.

AI assists—but the instructor decides.


**Instant Student Interaction**

Approved questions are delivered instantly to student dashboards through WebSockets. Students respond in real time, creating a dynamic classroom environment where participation becomes continuous rather than occasional.

**Live Analytics**

Spandan provides immediate insights into student engagement and understanding. Participation rates, response accuracy, and performance trends are visualized during the session itself, enabling instructors to adapt teaching on the fly.

## **The Impact**

Spandan transforms classrooms from passive environments into active learning ecosystems.

- Students shift from listening to thinking and responding  
- Instructors reduce cognitive load and focus on teaching  
- Engagement becomes continuous rather than episodic  
- Learning gaps are identified instantly, not after exams  

In pilot deployments, integrating real-time questioning significantly improved participation and correctness, especially when interaction was tied to engagement tracking.
