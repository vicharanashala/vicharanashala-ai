*Where questions find answers and knowledge grows with time,*  
*Vi-Sakha transforms support from reactive to continuously evolving.*


An initiative by Vicharanashala, IIT Ropar, Vi-Sakha is an AI-powered knowledge assistant designed to deliver timely, reliable support at scale. In academic environments, access to consistent support remains a persistent challenge. At institutions like IIT Ropar, students regularly depend on support systems for queries related to courses, processes, and logistics. However, these systems often struggle due to repetitive questions, limited bandwidth, and delays extending up to 24 to 48 hours.

---

## **About**

Vi-Sakha reimagines academic support as a continuously evolving knowledge ecosystem. Instead of a static chatbot, it combines verified human knowledge with AI-driven efficiency to create a system that becomes smarter with every interaction. The platform learns from past responses, captures new knowledge, and scales support without compromising quality or consistency.  

---

## **The Challenge**

In academic support, one persistent problem emerges:  
*How can institutions provide responsive, consistent support at scale when resources are limited?*

Traditional support systems face multiple obstacles:
- Repetitive questions consume valuable staff time  
- Knowledge is not captured or reused across interactions  
- Response delays extend from hours to days  
- Support quality is inconsistent across different staff members  
- Insights from past interactions are lost rather than accumulated  

The core challenge is not just answering questions—but **building a continuously evolving knowledge system that learns from every interaction and scales reliably**.

---

## **The Platform**

Vi-Sakha addresses this challenge through a trust-first architecture that prioritizes verified knowledge while enabling continuous learning.

**Trust-First Knowledge Retrieval**

Vi-Sakha is built on a core principle: verified knowledge is always prioritized over AI-generated responses. The system retrieves answers from structured, curated databases first. Only when no reliable information is available does it generate a response. This ensures that users receive answers grounded in context and validated institutional knowledge.

**Feedback-Driven Learning**

Every interaction contributes to system improvement. Unresolved or unsatisfactory responses are captured and reviewed by experts, allowing the system to continuously refine and expand its knowledge base. As more queries are processed, the knowledge base becomes richer and more comprehensive.

**Continuous Evolution**

With each verified answer added, the system becomes more self-sufficient. The reliance on AI-generated responses gradually decreases, transforming Vi-Sakha into a dependable and intelligent knowledge system aligned with institutional needs and continuously improving through accumulated expertise.  

---

## **The Impact**

Vi-Sakha has transformed support delivery and institutional efficiency:

- Queries previously requiring one to two days are now answered within minutes  
- Complex or escalated queries are addressed within a few hours  
- Support teams experience significantly reduced workload and cognitive burden  
- Response consistency has improved dramatically across all interactions  
- User satisfaction has increased through faster, more reliable support  

With over 1,200 active users and more than 1,300 queries resolved, the system has proven its ability to scale knowledge-driven support without proportional increases in staffing.

---

## **The Vision**

Vi-Sakha envisions support systems where:
- Knowledge is accumulated rather than lost with each interaction  
- Responses improve continuously through feedback and verification  
- AI enhances human expertise rather than replacing it  
- Support scales reliably without compromising quality  
- Systems become smarter and more reliable over time  

Beyond a single institution, Vi-Sakha's architecture extends across universities, educational platforms, and organizational knowledge systems. It can support multiple domains ranging from academic assistance to administrative workflows, with the strength of the validated knowledge database ensuring higher accuracy and reduced manual intervention.

Vi-Sakha represents a shift from static FAQ systems and overburdened support channels to dynamic, knowledge-driven ecosystems built on the principle that every interaction contributes to improvement.
