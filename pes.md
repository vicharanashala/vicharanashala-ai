*Where evaluation meets fairness and trust,*  
*PES ensures every assessment is reliable, transparent, and just.*

Developed at the Vicharanashala Lab, Indian Institute of Technology Ropar, PES is an intelligent peer evaluation system designed to enable scalable, anonymous, and reliable assessment. By combining automated analysis with structured human oversight, the platform ensures fairness, accountability, and academic integrity in peer-based evaluation environments.

---

## **About**

PES is a peer-to-peer evaluation system that facilitates anonymous assessment while maintaining quality control. Each student is assigned a unique identifier for every assessment, ensuring anonymity without losing traceability.

The system supports both offline and online submission workflows and integrates algorithm-based anomaly detection to monitor evaluation patterns. By combining automation with multi-level human review, PES creates a balanced ecosystem where peer evaluation becomes both scalable and trustworthy.

---

## **The Challenge**

In both academic and large-scale evaluation settings, one fundamental question arises:  
*How do we ensure fairness, consistency, and integrity when students evaluate each other?*

Traditional peer assessment systems face multiple challenges:
- Bias and inconsistency in grading  
- Lack of anonymity or lack of accountability  
- Difficulty in detecting unfair or manipulated evaluations  
- Limited mechanisms for quality control and oversight  

The core challenge is not just enabling peer evaluation—but **ensuring it is fair, transparent, and systematically validated**.

---

## **The Platform**

PES addresses this challenge through a structured evaluation pipeline that integrates anonymity, automation, and multi-level review.

**Anonymous Evaluation Framework**

Each student is assigned a unique ID per assessment, ensuring:
- Complete anonymity during evaluation  
- Secure mapping of submissions and reviewers  
- Traceability for audit and analysis  

**Flexible Submission Workflows**

PES supports two submission modes:

- **Bulk Download with QR Codes**  
  Teachers generate assessment templates with embedded QR codes, distribute them offline, and later upload scanned submissions mapped to student IDs.

- **Student Portal Upload**  
  Students upload submissions directly, with unique IDs generated automatically—no QR codes required.

**Peer Evaluation Process**

Students evaluate assigned submissions using predefined rubrics, ensuring structured and consistent feedback while maintaining anonymity.

**Anomaly Detection and Quality Control**

Statistical techniques such as outlier detection and deviation analysis identify suspicious evaluation patterns.

Flagged cases follow a multi-level escalation workflow:
- Automated detection  
- TA review  
- Teacher intervention  

**Aggregation and Analytics**

Validated evaluations are aggregated using statistical methods to generate fair final scores. The system also provides evaluation analytics, feedback insights, and complete audit trails.
