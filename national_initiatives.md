Vicharanashala operates at the intersection of pedagogical research and national educational policy. By leading the design and implementation of pivotal programs, the lab contributes to shaping a future where Indian higher education is both technologically advanced and deeply human-centric.

### Gurusetu: A National Mission for Faculty Excellence

Gurusetu is a flagship initiative of the Ministry of Education, Government of India, with three Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) serving as nodal centers. Vicharanashala is proud to be a key partner in this project, which serves as a cornerstone for faculty development under the Malaviya Mission.

The mission aims to move beyond traditional training by building a high-quality, open-access content repository for institutions across India. By making these resources freely available, the project envisions a personalized and flexible faculty development format, breaking away from rigid, traditional training methods to meet the evolving needs of modern educators.

Faculty members interested in the future of teaching can explore the vision and join the journey through the pilot portal. [Explore the Gurusetu Pilot]

### Capacity Building Program in Artificial Intelligence

Funded by the Ministry of Education, Government of India

We are at a crossroads where the landscape of learning is shifting beneath our feet. We live in an age where no conversation is complete without addressing Artificial Intelligence (AI), and higher education is no exception. While there is significant discourse regarding the impact of AI on education and the need to prepare students and teachers for this revolution, one question remains: with so many programs running nationwide, why choose this Faculty Development Program?

In our AI capacity-building programs, we pivot away from a "tools-first" approach. We believe that AI literacy is not merely about mastering prompts or new software; it is about learning how to think alongside a new kind of intelligence.

The Philosophy of Our Training

We treat AI as a pedagogical partner. This means focusing on the ethical weight of our choices, the critical evaluation of machine output, and, most importantly, maintaining a human-centered classroom. We do more than offer hands-on training with AI tools; we nudge our participants to reflect, question, and ultimately design their own unique approaches to teaching a generation that is growing up in a world where AI is fundamental to how they live, learn, and think.
