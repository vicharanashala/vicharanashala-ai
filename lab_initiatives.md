VicharanaShala works at the intersection of pedagogy, technology, and reflective practice. Our initiatives are designed to support educators, students, and institutions through meaningful learning innovation.

## **Global Speaker Series: A Dialogue Across Borders**

While the fundamental theories of education—from Bloom's Taxonomy to Project-Based Learning—remain universal, their application is deeply personal. At Vicharanashala, we recognize that every educator interprets these frameworks differently, allowing them to take a unique shape in every classroom.

The Global Speaker Series is our attempt to explore how these theories play out in classrooms outside India. It is a space designed to uncover the realities we share and the opportunities we have to learn from diverse perspectives, while offering our own insights to the collective "world classroom."

### **Moving Beyond the "Sage on the Stage"**

This is not a regular webinar series. We move away from the traditional model of experts delivering wisdom from a distance. Instead, we invite teaching faculty from global universities to share a theme, an idea, or an experiment they have tried in their own practice.

**The Flow:** The sessions are structured as natural dialogues. Knowledge isn't delivered; it emerges through the interaction between our guest speakers and our participant community.

**The Exchange:** We look for the "aha!" moments that happen when a practitioner from another part of the world shares a challenge or a success that resonates with our own local context.

**The Objective:** To build a global bridge of practice where we learn, unlearn, and relearn together.

## **V-Talks: Vicharana in Action**

At the heart of our lab is the spirit of Vicharana—the act of deep reflection and inquiry. V-Talks (Vicharanashala Talks) are the living embodiment of this spirit. We believe that insightful conversations shouldn't be confined to the walls of academia.

### **A Crossroads for Diverse Perspectives**

V-Talks bring together people from different walks of life. We invite professionals, creators, and thinkers to discuss themes that resonate far beyond the classroom.

**For Everyone:** While these topics are vital for our students and faculty, they are designed for anyone curious about the world. Whether you are part of a university or have nothing to do with academia, V-Talks offer a space to engage with ideas that affect our shared human experience.

**Dialogue in Motion:** These are not static presentations. True to the spirit of the lab, V-Talks are about interaction. It is about the spark that happens when a diverse audience questions, reflects, and interacts with a speaker's lived experience.

**Vicharana in Action:** We pick general, yet profound, topics and explore them through the lens of active reflection.

### **Join the Conversation**

Vicharanashala is a community-driven space, and we want every V-Talk to reflect the interests of the people it serves.

**Interested in Speaking?** If you are a professional with a unique perspective or a story that sparks reflection, we invite you to take the stage.

**Nominate a Voice:** Is there someone whose ideas have made you pause and think? Nominate a speaker from any field to lead our next V-Talk.
